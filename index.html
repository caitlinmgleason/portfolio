DESIGN

Botanica

Design: Caitlin Gleason

Branding project for a conceptual conservatory and plant shop which would serve as a resource for education, community, and botanical necessities. Taking inspiration from, and incorporating, the illustrations of Ernst Haeckel, I brought together concept imagery and interior/exterior mockups to create the desired brand image. Using this direction, I designed stationary for the shop and packaging for some products it may offer.

H2GO

Design: Caitlin Gleason

Product design and branding for a customizable water bottle to suit the needs of the city traveler. Water bottle would include a strap for portability, a moisture-resistant sleeve to avoid condensation, the accessibility of interchangeable lids, and various ad-ons such as a mini filter or straw. Branding includes packaging of product and promotional flyers and stickers that highlight the benefits of staying hydrated and the ease of personalization.

Louboutin Rouge

Design: Caitlin Gleason

Packaging design and branding for a proposed lipstick to accompany the existing Christian Louboutin nail polish and shoe lines. Inspired by Freedom Tower in New York City, the packaging and branding is meant to elevate this luxury product in a similar fashion as the unique bottle design for Louboutin’s extravagant nail polish. Deliverables include mockups for lipstick and case, potential advertisements, and outlines for target consumers.

Chicago Public Schools

Design: Caitlin Gleason

Project for capstone class at Columbia College Chicago focused on creating design solutions based on research of packaging design trends. Comparing popular food and snack packaging against those specifically marketed to be “healthy,” I integrated their color and design elements with the goal of creating graphic packaging to promote healthy eating in Chicago public schools. Illustrations in collaboration with Theresa Potts .

Box of Awesome

Design: Caitlin Gleason

Conceptual new product/rebrand for Wendy’s fast food. Imagined as a “kids meal for adults” it is a combo meal with a prize inside engineered to appeal to the teen and young adult demographics. Project included Brand strategy as laid out in a branding book, design of the product logo (incorporating Wendy’s brand logo), and design of box and accessories.
